Who

I'm Katherine. The "K4" in my handle is a long story stretching back to 2010. Other stories stretch back to a college hacker society, foam swords, creeks and a few orchestra camps.

I grew up outside Buffalo, NY, headed to Cleveland for school, and landed in San Francisco in 2017. I've been here since.

Outside of work, I fill my time with music (mostly bluegrass right now, though I have some decent classical and latin repertoire), swinging longswords at F3 Sword Academy in Oakland, or gardening with my chickens.

I recently wrapped up as Board President at California Music Center (2018-2024), invest through Portfolia's Femtech III fund, and am currently applying some structured learning around agentic AI from The Multiverse School.

I care a lot about mentorship, especially for women in tech. I've been doing this long enough to have Opinions about sustainable careers, and I'm always happy to chat over coffee about navigating this industry: Connect on LinkedIn
